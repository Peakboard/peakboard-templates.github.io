title: Handheld application for maintenance and repair at the machine

description: 
 - This template was specifically developed for use on industrial handheld devices and tablets and supports employees in maintenance and servicing processes directly at the machine. Access to the respective equipment is easily achieved via a QR code, which is scanned using the device’s integrated scanner. This ensures that the relevant information and functions are instantly available.

 - The application provides targeted troubleshooting guides to efficiently resolve issues. At the same time, maintenance logs can be created and documented directly on site. Spare parts management is also integrated, allowing parts to be booked and tracked so that all relevant information is centralized in one place. In addition, the template enables users to review past maintenance activities, creating full transparency of completed actions.

 - The template is ideal for companies looking to digitize their maintenance processes and support employees with an intuitive mobile solution directly within the production environment.
