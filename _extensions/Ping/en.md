  - The Ping extension lets your Peakboard board check the reachability of one or many network devices at every data refresh. Enter a single IP address or hostname, or a comma-separated list, and receive a clean two-column result set with the device and its current status (OK if it responds, NOK if it does not). It's the fastest way to turn a Peakboard into a live network and equipment availability board.
